About Me

My interests lie in alternative computational models for artificial intelligence, mass social patterns, chaotic/emergent systems, and linguistics. My current research is focused on novel ways of scaling deep neural networks through neurologically inspired modularity. Questions such as: How can we reduce parameter space, increase interpretability, and create self-similar task divisions like the brain?


I am a 2024 Foresight Institute Fellow in Neurotechnology. You can find the press release on the Foresight Institute's website.


I am also the Co-Founder of Myelin Neurotech, a nonprofit working on neurotechnology research. You can find our work on the Myelin Neurotech page.
